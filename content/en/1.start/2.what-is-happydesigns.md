happydesigns builds modern web systems with Nuxt, Nuxt UI, Nuxt Content, Docus, and agent-assisted development workflows.

The ecosystem is built around reusable layers and products:

- `ui` provides reusable interface and content foundations.
- `id` is the intended identity and brand-layer system.
- `brand` is the current concrete happydesigns brand layer.
- `business` is the Business OS product direction.
- `nuxt-variants` supports typed layout and page variants.
- `docs` is this ecosystem operating manual.
- `help` is the future customer-facing help center.

The goal is consistency without freezing the system. Humans and agents should be able to understand where a change belongs, reuse existing foundations, and keep product architecture intact.

## Not a marketing site

These docs describe how the happydesigns ecosystem works. They avoid vague service claims, unstable implementation notes, and customer support material that belongs in future help docs.
