`happydesigns/identity` is the product direction for the generic identity and brand-layer system.

These docs should explain what identity means for the ecosystem. They should not become the full product documentation for building with `happydesigns/identity`.

Document strategy here. Document implementation in the dedicated identity product docs.

## Scope

This ecosystem manual owns the cross-product identity boundary:

- The relationship between `ui`, `identity`, `brand`, and client identities.
- What brand layers may and may not own.
- The token hierarchy used across happydesigns.
- The current status of `happydesigns/brand`.

The dedicated identity docs should own implementation material:

- Package installation and usage.
- Token schema reference.
- Brand-layer scaffolding.
- Migration guides.
- Examples and templates.
- Versioned implementation reference.

## Not the same as brand

`happydesigns/brand` is a concrete implementation. `happydesigns/identity` is the broader product direction for creating reusable identity systems for happydesigns and clients.
