Nuxt layers are used for presentation and composition.

## Purpose

Use layers when a concern should be composed into a Nuxt app as UI, content, layout, or brand expression.

## Decision rule

Layers adapt how a product appears and is composed. They do not own core product behavior.

## Use layers for

- UI foundations.
- Branding.
- Layouts.
- Pages.
- Components.
- App config.
- Public assets.
- Customer presentation.

## Do not use layers for

- Core product behavior.
- Authorization.
- Tenant isolation.
- Financial rules.
- Audit behavior.
- Runtime integration ownership.

## Examples

- `happydesigns/ui` is a reusable interface layer.
- `happydesigns/brand` is a concrete brand layer.
- A client identity layer can provide logos, tokens, metadata, and component defaults.

Brand and customer layers may adapt how a product looks and reads. They must not redefine what the product is allowed to do.
