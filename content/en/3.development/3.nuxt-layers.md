# Nuxt layers

Nuxt layers are used for presentation and composition.

## Use layers for

- UI foundations.
- Branding.
- Layouts.
- Pages.
- Components.
- App config.
- Public assets.
- Customer presentation.

## Do not use layers for

- Core product behavior.
- Authorization.
- Tenant isolation.
- Financial rules.
- Audit behavior.
- Runtime integration ownership.

Brand and customer layers may adapt how a product looks and reads. They should not redefine what the product is allowed to do.
