Nuxt layers are used for presentation and composition.

Use layers when a concern belongs in a Nuxt app as UI, content, layout, or brand expression.

Layers adapt how a product appears and is composed. They do not own core product behavior.

## Layer scope

Use layers for presentation and app composition:

- UI foundations.
- Branding.
- Layouts.
- Pages.
- Components.
- App config.
- Public assets.
- Customer presentation.

Do not use layers for product permissions or server-side ownership:

- Core product behavior.
- Authorization.
- Tenant isolation.
- Financial rules.
- Audit behavior.
- Runtime integration behavior.

## Examples

- `happydesigns/ui` is a reusable interface layer.
- `happydesigns/brand` is the happydesigns-specific brand layer.
- A client identity layer can provide logos, tokens, metadata, and component defaults.

Brand and customer layers adapt presentation. Product behavior stays outside them.
