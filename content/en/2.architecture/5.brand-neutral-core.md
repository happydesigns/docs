Reusable product cores should not depend on a specific customer or happydesigns visual identity.

## For Business OS

- The base product should use Nuxt UI as a calm, clear, efficient operations tool.
- The default experience should not depend on a specific customer brand.
- Branding is delivered through Nuxt layers.
- Brand layers must not provide invoice numbering, E-Rechnung rules, authorization, audit behavior, storage behavior, or domain model changes.

## For `ui`

- `ui` remains a reusable foundation.
- It may define structure and default composition patterns.
- Brand-specific color systems, logos, motifs, and visual identity live above it.

## For `id` / `brand`

Identity layers provide brand expression. They should be mostly declarative and compositional:

- Tokens.
- Logos.
- Metadata.
- App config.
- Nuxt UI defaults.
- Brand primitives.

They should not own app-specific business logic.
