Reusable product cores do not depend on a specific customer or happydesigns visual identity.

Brand neutrality keeps `ui`, Business OS, and shared product logic reusable. Identity layers can make a product feel specific without changing product behavior.

Branding adapts expression. Product cores own behavior.

## For Business OS

- The base product uses Nuxt UI as a calm, efficient operations tool.
- The default experience does not depend on a specific customer brand.
- Branding arrives through Nuxt layers.
- Brand layers theme the product shell. They do not change permissions, workflows, data rules, integrations, storage, or runtime behavior.

## For `ui`

- `ui` remains a reusable interface foundation.
- Brand-specific color systems, logos, motifs, and visual identity live above it.
- UI defaults may exist, but they must not become a specific brand.

## For identity and brand

Identity layers are mostly configuration. They define tokens, logos, metadata, app config, Nuxt UI defaults, docs styling, and brand primitives. They do not own application-specific product logic.
