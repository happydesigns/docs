Reusable product cores should not depend on a specific customer or happydesigns visual identity.

## Purpose

Brand neutrality keeps `ui`, Business OS, and shared product logic reusable. Identity layers can make a product feel specific without changing what the product is allowed to do.

## Decision rule

Branding adapts expression. Product cores own behavior.

## For Business OS

- The base product should use Nuxt UI as a calm, efficient operations tool.
- The default experience should not depend on a specific customer brand.
- Branding should arrive through Nuxt layers.
- Brand layers must not provide invoice numbering, E-Rechnung rules, authorization, audit behavior, storage behavior, or domain model changes.

## For `ui`

- `ui` remains a reusable interface foundation.
- Brand-specific color systems, logos, motifs, and visual identity live above it.
- UI defaults may exist, but they must not become a concrete brand.

## For identity and brand

Identity layers should be declarative and compositional. They define tokens, logos, metadata, app config, Nuxt UI defaults, docs styling, and brand primitives. They do not own application-specific business logic.
