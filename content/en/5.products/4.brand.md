`brand` is the happydesigns-specific brand layer. It applies the happydesigns visual system above reusable UI and product foundations.

Treat `brand` as the happydesigns implementation, not the reusable identity system.

## Product scope

The brand layer owns happydesigns presentation:

- happydesigns color roles.
- Tailwind theme tokens.
- Nuxt UI color-role mappings.
- CSS variables.
- Typography defaults.
- Logo and brand primitives.
- Docus styling and visual QA pages.

Use `brand` to theme brand-neutral products such as docs, product shells, and happydesigns-owned sites. The product keeps its own workflows, permissions, data rules, integrations, storage, and runtime behavior.
