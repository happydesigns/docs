`happydesigns/identity` is the generic identity and brand-layer product direction.

## Purpose

The product should define how brand layers are created, structured, documented, and applied on top of reusable Nuxt UI foundations.

## Decision rule

Use this page as an ecosystem overview only. Dedicated identity docs should own implementation.

## Owns

- Generic brand-layer model.
- Token schema direction.
- Scaffolding and templates.
- Identity implementation guides.
- Reusable examples for happydesigns and clients.

## Does not own here

This site does not document full APIs, package usage, templates, migrations, or versioned reference material for identity.

## Relationship to `brand`

`brand` currently exists as the concrete happydesigns brand implementation. `identity` is the broader product direction for creating reusable identity systems.
