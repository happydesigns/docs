`happydesigns/identity` is the generic identity and brand-layer product direction.

The product defines how brand layers are created, structured, documented, and applied on top of reusable Nuxt UI foundations.

Use this page as an ecosystem role summary only. Dedicated identity docs own implementation.

## Product scope

The identity product direction owns reusable brand-layer infrastructure:

- Generic brand-layer model.
- Token schema direction.
- Scaffolding and templates.
- Identity implementation guides.
- Reusable examples for happydesigns and clients.

This site does not document full APIs, package usage, templates, migrations, or versioned reference material for identity.

## Relationship to `brand`

`brand` is the concrete happydesigns brand implementation. `identity` is the broader product direction for creating reusable identity systems.
