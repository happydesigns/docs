The product map explains what each happydesigns product owns and where deeper documentation belongs.

Use this page when deciding whether a topic belongs in the ecosystem operating manual or in a dedicated product documentation site.

This site explains product roles and boundaries. Dedicated product docs explain implementation.

| Product / repo | Package or product identity | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `happydesigns/ui` | `@happydesigns/ui` | Interface and content foundation. |
| `happydesigns/identity` | Product direction | Generic identity and brand-layer system. |
| `happydesigns/brand` | `@happydesigns/brand` | Concrete happydesigns brand layer. |
| `happydesigns/business` | `@happydesigns/business` | Business OS product and workflow system. |
| `happydesigns/nuxt-variants` | `@happydesigns/nuxt-variants` | Typed layout and content variant module. |
| `happydesigns/docs` | `docs.happydesigns.de` | Ecosystem operating manual, MCP, LLM, and skills source. |
| `happydesigns/help` | Help center | Customer-facing website guidance. |

## Documentation boundary

Product pages in this section are overviews. They should not become API references, changelogs, or implementation manuals.
